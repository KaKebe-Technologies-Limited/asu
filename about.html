About Us

Who We Are

The Acholi Students Union (ASU) is a non-political, non-religious organization dedicated to fostering unity, peace, and prosperity among Acholi students across Uganda. Founded with the vision of creating a dignified, rational, and responsible Acholi society, we strive to empower youth through academic excellence, cultural preservation, and social transformation.

Promoting Acholi culture and identity.

Advocating for education and youth empowerment.

Building sustainable peace and unity.
