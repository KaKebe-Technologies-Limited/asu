About Us

A Dignified, Rational, and Responsible Acholi Society

The Acholi Students Union (ASU) is a non-political, non-religious organization dedicated to promoting academic excellence, unity, and social transformation for Acholi students across Uganda.

Promoting Acholi culture and identity.

Advocating for education and youth empowerment.

Fostering unity and sustainable peace.
