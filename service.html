What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects & Activities

The Acholi Students Union is committed to empowering students through impactful projects and activities that promote education, unity, cultural preservation, and social development across Uganda.

Scholarship Program

Providing financial support to deserving Acholi students to pursue higher education and achieve academic excellence.

Cultural Festivals

Organizing events to celebrate Acholi heritage through music, dance, and storytelling, fostering cultural pride.

Peacebuilding Workshops

Career Mentorship
